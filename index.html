About   Me

Hello There! Thank you for visiting! My Name is Jaime Sandoval and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in Orange County, California.

I truly enjoy using the combination of imagination and logic to create applications for people to interact with. Whether the coding involves implementing an interesting API, debugging, or solving an algorithm, I'm all for it.

While attending Learning Fuze Coding Boot camp, I was immersed in technologies used for building Full Stack Applications. I used those very technologies to build the projects featured in this portfolio! Learning Fuze gave me a solid foundation from which to work with, and I've been busy putting that foundation to work ever since.

Staying up to date with current dev trends is another passion of mine. I do that in a variety of ways, such as attending tech meetups, listening to dev podcasts and finding interesting Software Engineers to follow on Twitter.

I'd love to bring my skills to a company and I look forward to being part of a Dev Team. If you'd like to contact me please feel free to do so, so that we can make something incredible with code.
