About Me

Coding has become my life. If I go a day without coding it feels odd, and that's mainly because it's such a big part of me.

I truly enjoy using the combination of my imagination and logic to create something new and useful for people, or to use the skills I've acquired to find a solution to a problem. Whether it's debugging or solving an algorithm, I'm game. I wasn't always involved with tech, but I took the plunge at the suggestion of a softball teammate, to whom I'm forever grateful towards for introducing me to this field. Ever since I took his suggestion, I've never looked back.

When I'm not developing I like to spend my time doing outdoor activities such as snowboarding, surfing, hiking or just a playing pick up game of basketball with friends. Staying up to date with current dev trends is another passion of mine. I do that in a variety of ways, such as attending tech meetups, listening to dev podcasts and finding interesting Software Engineers to follow on Twitter.

I'd love to bring my skills to a company and I look forward to being part of a Dev Team. Let's collaborate and make something incredible with code and team work!
